Getting Started

Getting Started

Logging In

Your content management system (CMS) is where you edit everything on qolc.latzwebdesign.com. To access it:

  1. Go to your studio URL: qolc-coaching.sanity.studio/structure
  2. Log in with the account you were invited with (Google, GitHub, or email/password).
  3. You'll land on the main editing screen. Your content sections are listed in the left sidebar under "Home Page."

The Two Tabs

At the top of your studio you'll see two tabs:

Edit Content Your main workspace. Browse and edit all content here. This is where you'll spend most of your time.
Live Preview See your actual website with changes updating as you type. Click any element in the preview to jump directly to that field in the editor.

Drafts vs. Published

Every change you make is saved as a draft automatically. Drafts are not visible on the live website — only you can see them in the studio.

To make your changes live, click the Publish button at the bottom right of the editor. Changes appear on qolc.latzwebdesign.com within about a minute.

💡 Tip: Don't worry about breaking anything. Your drafts don't affect the live site until you hit Publish. Edit freely, then publish when it looks right.

Working with Images

To add or change an image:

  1. Click the image field.
  2. Click "Upload" to select a file from your computer, or drag and drop.
  3. Set a hotspot by clicking on the image and dragging the circle to the most important part. This ensures the image crops well on all screen sizes.
  • Use JPG or PNG format.
  • Keep file sizes under 2MB for best performance.
  • Always set the hotspot on your headshot — put it on your face.
  • The system automatically optimizes images for fast loading.

Publishing

  1. Make your edits.
  2. Use Live Preview if you want to check how things look first.
  3. Click "Publish" at the bottom right.
  4. Wait about a minute, then check the live site. If changes don't appear, do a hard refresh: Ctrl+Shift+R on PC or Cmd+Shift+R on Mac.
💡 Tip: Bookmark your studio URL so you can get back quickly: qolc-coaching.sanity.studio/structure

Quick Tips

✅Always publishDrafts don't show on the live site. Hit Publish when you're done.
🎯Set hotspotsOn every image — especially your headshot. Put it on your face.
✂️Keep text conciseShort, clear messaging converts visitors into clients. Less is more.
🚫Don't leave fields emptyBlank fields can create awkward gaps on the page.
⏱️Changes take ~1 minuteAfter publishing, give it a moment. Hard refresh if needed: Ctrl+Shift+R / Cmd+Shift+R
✏️Inline formattingIn the Introduction section: _underscores_ = italic, *asterisks* = bold.
📱Yellow warnings vs. red errorsYellow limit warnings are suggestions — content still publishes. Red errors must be fixed first.
🔒Anchor IDs are lockedIn Navigation, only edit the Label — never the Anchor ID.
